About Us

A Distinct Identity

Single Mothers Matter is a 501(c)(3) organization driven by a single goal; to do our part in making the world a better place by providing resources, support, and development to single parent families. We understand that the challenges of single parent house holds are numerous. Consequently, we strive to build strong families and communities by creating supportive relationships and making a positive impact with all of our pursuits. We are in the process of of raising funds to launch services in January 2019.

This vision was given from God to single mother Shonda Anthony who believes that when families are provided the inspiration, resources and support needed that they can become and have all that God intended for them. Shonda and her family experienced a number of challenges over the years to include health, abuse, and homelessness. However, they overcame every challenge with the help of God and others.

It is our philosophy that single mothers have dreams and ambitions just like everyone else, but are often not able to pursue them because they must focus their attention and resources on providing for their children. Single Mothers Matter encourages, inspires, and assists mothers in their pursuits by providing coaching, skill development and leadership opportunities in effort that career goals, passions, and life purpose are attainable. It is our belief that single parent families will thrive and grow stronger when provided the inspiration, support, tools, and resources needed to fulfill their dreams.

Misson & Vision

Mission

To provide supportive services, skill training, and development to single mothers in effort to help them create self-sufficiency for their families.

Vision

Single mothers are equipped to be financially independent, empowering caregivers, living purposeful lives and thriving as leaders.

Values

Empowerment

Our desire is to empower every mother and child that we serve to grow and develop utilizing the tools that we provide in effort to live their best lives. Everyone connected to our organization is empowered to utilize their skills and talents to help us fulfill our mission.

Love

This is our foundation. We will be patient, kind, and selfless to those that we serve and partner with. We will not be prideful in our service neither envious of the service of others. We will not behave rudely and we will hope, believe, bear and endure all things.

Community

It takes a village to help support single mothers and their children. We always strive to partner with existing nonprofits, businesses, and individuals within our communities because we recognize that everyone has a part to contribute to this great assignment.

Integrity

We pursue honorable initiatives and are transparent and ethical in everything that we do.

Inclusion

We do not discriminate against race, religion, sexual orientation, or any other discriminatory profiling.
